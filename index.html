I'm a tinkerer and problem-solver who just enjoys building from scratch.

Sometimes my projects work out almost, but not quite, totally unlike what I imagined. That's why when people ask what I’m working on, I often reply “just stuff.” It’s my code for “I'm not really sure how this will work out, so I don't want to say yet".

Sometimes they don't. This blog is an eclectic assemblage of those. Like me, it's quite varied: a mine of useless information, vaguely coherent code snippets, woodwork projects, puzzles, cooking experiments that are (mostly) edible and photos of places I've been and things I've seen. I hope you enjoy it.
