---
title: The FitDays App
date: 2025-12-08
---
I am working on getting a bit slimmer, but manually recording my
weight and squabbling with a scale that sometime over-reads by 2kg 
is getting just a bit tiresome.  And in the bathroom we have two
scales anyway - one that I use and one that wife uses to link 
with the app that is helping her.  A single scale would just be
so much easier.  And an app that connects to that scale easier 
still.  There is no shortage of apps that claim to do that and I
spent the weekend going from one to another but finally settled 
on one called FitDays.  It links with the scale and actually has
more functionality than the app that came with the scale.  That app
just records weight, but the scale actually has bio-impedance 
measuring and can provide a lot of other information too.  It's 
free and works well for me.  Let's see if in three months I'm 
still enamoured with it.
