title: Fitdays App
date: 2025-12-08
---
I am working on getting a bit slimmer, but manually recording my
weight and squabbling with a scale that sometime over-reads by 2kg 
is getting just a bit tiresome.  And in the bathroom we have two
scales anyway - the difficult one that I use and an easy one that 
my wife uses to link with the app that is helping her.  A single 
scale would just be so much easier.  And an app that connects to 
that scale easier still.  There is no shortage of apps that claim 
to do that and I spent the weekend going from one to another but 
finally settled on one called Fitdays.  It links with the scale 
and actually has more functionality than the app that the scale 
came with. That app just records weight, but the scale actually 
has bio-impedance measuring and can provide a lot of other 
information too.  Fitdays accessess all that extra date. It's 
free and works well for me.  Let's see if in three months I'm 
still enamoured with it.
