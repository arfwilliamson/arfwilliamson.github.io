---
categories: Recipes
title: "Shortcrust Pastry"
prep_time: "15 mins"
servings: "4 pies"
ingredients:
  - 1 1/2 c flour (200g)
  - pinch salt
  - 6 T oil
  - 1/4 c water
  - 1/2 t bicarb
---

- Knife together dry ingredients and oil
  until the consistency of bread crumbs.
- Add water and knead.
- Allow to rest overnight in sealed 
  container.
- Roll out on floured counter top.
