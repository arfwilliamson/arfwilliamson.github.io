---
categories: Recipes
title: "Easy Paella"
prep_time: "15 mins"
servings: "4"
ingredients:
  - 1 T oil
  - 1 chopped onion (and pepper)
  - 1 t paprika
  - 1 t thyme
  - 3 T white wine (optional)
  - 400 g tomatoes
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 900 ml chicken stock
  - 400 g seafood or chorizo
  - 1/2 lemon squeezed
  - 300 g rice
  - parsley
---

Fry onion.\
Add herbs, spices, wine, rice and dry out.\
Add tomatoes and stock.\
Cook until rice is done.\
Add seafood or chorizo, lemon juice\
